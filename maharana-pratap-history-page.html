Maharana Pratap




Maharana Pratap (1540–1597)


was a brave Rajput king of Mewar, Rajasthan.

He is remembered for his courage, patriotism, and resistance against the Mughal emperor Akbar.

The historic

Battle of Haldighati (1576) iis a symbol of his bravery,

where he fought fearlessly with his loyal horse Chetak.

Maharana Pratap never accepted Akbar’s rule and continued his struggle for freedom. He lived a simple life, dedicated to protecting the independence of Mewar, and is celebrated as a symbol of Rajput pride and valor.

Quick Facts

  • Born: 9 May 1540
  • Famous Battle: Haldighati (1576)
  • Horse: Chetak
  • Died: 19 January 1597
